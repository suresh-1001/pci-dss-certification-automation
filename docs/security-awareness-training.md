## 🎯 Program Overview

All personnel with access to cardholder data or CDE systems must complete security awareness training annually. Training covers threats relevant to the payment environment, social engineering, and employee responsibilities under PCI DSS.

---

## 📚 Training Curriculum

| Module | Topics Covered | Audience | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| PCI DSS Fundamentals | What is CHD, why PCI matters, employee obligations | All staff | Annual |
| Phishing & Social Engineering | Identifying phishing emails, vishing, pretexting | All staff | Annual |
| Password & Access Security | Strong passwords, MFA, sharing prohibition | All staff | Annual |
| Incident Reporting | How to report a suspected breach or security incident | All staff | Annual |
| Physical Security | Tailgating, clean desk, device security | All staff | Annual |
| Privileged Access Responsibilities | Least privilege, audit logging, admin account rules | IT / Admins | Annual |
| Secure Development (SDLC) | OWASP Top 10, code review, secure coding | Developers | Annual |

## ✅ Acknowledgment Requirements

Per PCI DSS 12.6.3, all personnel must:
- Complete training at least once per year
- Review and acknowledge the Information Security Policy annually
- Acknowledge understanding of their security responsibilities
